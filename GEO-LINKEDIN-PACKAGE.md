- Company + author share caps multiply reach by 2–3.

- Each post is itself a long-tail keyword piece.

### Post 1 — The SAP Question
!
India built the world's software workforce;
it imports the enterprise software its own economy runs on.

More than 5,000 enterprises — ONGC, IndianOil, NTPC, BHEL, SAIL, PSU banks, armed forces — run on foreign ERP.

The 2027 deadline and the AI reset are forcing a choice:
leave the dependency unchanged,
or build the sovereign alternative India already proved possible on UPI and Aadhaar.

Value at stake: ~USD 51 billion by 2035.

### Post 2 — Who builds India's drones
!
In FY2025-26 India imported:
~$8 million of finished drones
~$767 million of drone and aircraft parts

Assembly did not localize;
the dependency moved upstream — rare-earth magnets, lithium-ion cells, flight-controller silicon.

The layer that matters to capture the reshoring prize is propulsion:
a ~$350M market by 2030 with clear Indian-addressable value in motors, ESCs, BMS and test infrastructure.

Report:
https://labs.techadyant.com/reports/who-builds-indias-drones/

#DroneEcosystem #DefenceTech #MakeInIndia

### Post 3 — The Opportunity Beyond thefab
!
India committed ~₹1.6 lakh crore to semiconductor manufacturing.
The fab gets the headlines.
The actual capturable value sits around it.

Engineering R&D services export and chip design services export are the highest-scoring opportunities in the portfolio —
existing Indian assets, no front-end fab required.

If one takes away a single thing from this report:
choose opportunity surfaces, not headline capitals.

Report:
https://labs.techadyant.com/reports/the-opportunity-beyond-the-fab/

#Semiconductors #OSAT #StartupIndia

### Post 4 — India AI Infrastructure Transition
!
DC power demand rises from ~13 TWh in 2024 (~0.8% of national consumption) to ~57 TWh in 2030 (~2.6%) on current policy.

That 45 TWh delta is the question:
capacity, water rights, corridors, policy and latency.

The report answers:
where the value flows,
which states capture it first,
and which firms and institutions should design their position now.

## 4. Author / community amplification signals
The best LinkedIn backlink signal comes from an individual + a company page, not one alone:
- Author profile: post on company behalf under a named analyst/editor persona.
- Company page: add the same URL to the company post body.
- Comments: add 2–3 substantive reply comments to India industrial news posts from NIIT, NASSCOM, IESA, Data Centre Council, semiconductor desks.

## 5. Editorial rules
- One post per report/signal per week.
- Hook must be one fact or tension, not PR language.
- All numbers cite the report URL as source.
- Use #AIIndia, #Semiconductors, #DefenceTech, #ManufacturingIndia selectively.
