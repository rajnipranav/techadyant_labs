The moat

What AI coding assistants still can't do — and what we sell.

Pricing premium tiers at premium rates is only credible if we can articulate why the work is not commodity. These five areas are genuinely hard, and clients pay for the judgment.

  • Messy real-world integrationsCRMs, ERPs, banking APIs, government portals, WooCommerce plugin ecosystems — poorly documented and inconsistently behaved.
  • Reliability & governanceSecurity, compliance, human-in-the-loop safeguards that stop agents making expensive mistakes in production.
  • Strategic designTranslating vague business goals into robust agent workflows with clear inputs, outputs and failure modes.
  • Domain judgmentKnowing which 80% of the work an agent should handle and which 20% must stay human — that line moves by industry.
  • Ongoing operationsEvals, drift monitoring, token-cost control, guardrail maintenance, model-migration as foundation models keep changing.
